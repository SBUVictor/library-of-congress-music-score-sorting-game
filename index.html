Welcome to Shelf It Right

Think you can outsmart the shelves? 📚🎼

Shelf It Right uses Library of Congress Classification call numbers for music scores, not Dewey Decimal numbers. It drops you into the logic of real music library call numbers, where every decimal, letter, Cutter, and Roman numeral matters. Learn how librarians compare and organize scores using authentic-looking Library of Congress call numbers before stepping into challenge rounds, quiz battles, and the final drag-and-drop showdown.

Train your brain. Trust the logic. Beat the stack.
